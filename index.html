The short version

WispNote does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no internet connection required — ever.

01

Who we are

WispNote is a Windows desktop application developed and published by Digital Dilemma. This privacy policy applies to the WispNote application distributed via the Microsoft Store.

If you have any questions about this policy, please use the contact details at the bottom of this page.

02

We collect no personal data whatsoever. WispNote is a fully offline application.

03

Data stored on your device

WispNote stores the following data locally on your computer only. This data never leaves your device.

Your note content, window positions, theme preferences, and app settings are saved in a local application data folder on your PC. This data is accessible only to you and is not shared with Digital Dilemma or any third party.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling WispNote. Uninstallation removes all app data from your system.

04

Microsoft Store

WispNote is distributed via the Microsoft Store. Microsoft may collect certain data as part of the Store platform — such as download counts, crash reports, and purchase records — in accordance with Microsoft's own Privacy Policy.

Digital Dilemma does not receive any personally identifiable information from Microsoft as part of this distribution arrangement.

05

Children's privacy

WispNote does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As no data is collected at all, the app is inherently compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

06

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes in a future version of WispNote, the updated policy will be published at this URL with a revised effective date. We will not make changes that reduce your privacy protections without clear notice.

Given that WispNote collects no data, meaningful changes to this policy are unlikely.
